About

I hold a degree in Biomedical Engineering from the University of Engineering and Technology, Lahore, and I'm a registered engineer with the Pakistan Engineering Council. Somewhere between the lab bench and the lecture hall, I found that the work I cared most about was helping people understand how things work — which is what led me into teaching.

Since 2022, I've taught Science across three institutions, most recently carrying two concurrent roles: a Science Teacher Internship with the Punjab School Education Department, and a Lead Educator position at ACE Education Academy, where I teach Science, Biology, Chemistry, and Computer Science to Classes 6–10.

My engineering background shapes how I teach — I bring structured problem-solving and a systems mindset to lesson design, and I'm building toward a role in an international school in the Saudi Arabia, Oman, UAE or Qatar.
